# GGD REQUIREMENTS

- `REQ-GGD-001`
  The system shall treat layout as a deterministic geometry program with declared constants, route roles, and viewport-dependent rules.

- `REQ-GGD-002`
  The system shall maintain dual-write state in `GGD/STATE.md` and `GGD/state.json`.

- `REQ-GGD-003`
  The system shall lock geometry, typography, color, semantic, and code-quality conventions in one authoritative contract.

- `REQ-GGD-004`
  The system shall define route-role-specific acceptance tests for `home`, `flagship`, and `product-family`.

- `REQ-GGD-005`
  The system shall map existing mockups and live routes before new autonomous execution is trusted.

- `REQ-GGD-006`
  The system shall classify verification findings as `CRITICAL`, `MAJOR`, `MINOR`, or `NOTE`.

- `REQ-GGD-007`
  The system shall reject any route candidate that introduces new critical or major geometry, semantic, truth, or code-quality regressions.

- `REQ-GGD-008`
  The system shall keep GitHub-derived packet truth authoritative for repo-backed product pages.

- `REQ-GGD-009`
  The system shall preserve `/imc` as a flagship route with explicit non-generic exceptions.

- `REQ-GGD-010`
  The system shall prove the shared product-family kernel on at least two distinct repo pages before broader rollout.

- `REQ-GGD-011`
  The system shall support bounded recursive improvement loops with explicit recovery and rejection behavior.

- `REQ-GGD-012`
  The system shall not claim unattended autonomy readiness until the GGD contract, verification, and active route state are aligned.
